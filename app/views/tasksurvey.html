#GoodVibes Introduction Letter

Hello,

Thank you for applying to the GoodVibes Brand Ambassador Program. We are very excited to have you on board and on behalf of everyone at Viber, not only in India but globally, we would like to welcome you aboard.

By downloading and using Viber, you will be a part of 360 million Viber worldwide user base, including 20 million in India. Viber is developed by Viber Media, a pioneering mobile messaging and VoIP company. Viber users can send free (operator data charges may apply) text messages, fun stickers, photos, videos and doodles, share locations anywhere in the world, make free HD-quality calls, communicate with Push-To-Talk. Viber seamlessly integrates between Mobiles, Tablets and Desktops and is available across different mobile and desktop platforms.Viber is simple to use - it integrates effortlessly with your existing address book and uses your cell phone number as your Viber ID.

As a GoodVibes ambassador, you would be working on tasks that are going to be full of learning and of course, fun. We are sure you have lots of questions and rest assured, you will discover more as you progress through the various stages of the program. We would like to emphasize that it is one of the first and most important programs for the Viber India Office. Everything that you do as a part of this program represents and projects Viber as a brand and so, we have kept not only Viber but also you at the heart of every activity.

Therefore, GoodVibes isn’t merely an ambassador program for us; we intend to create a life-long connection with you and we’re going to try our best to make this a fruitful experience for you. To further instil the spirit of GoodVibes, we’ll be giving away exciting rewards like the latest gadgets, trips across India and even a place on the GoodVibes World Tour to the top performer.

We take this opportunity to wish you the very best! Go ahead and spread the Good Vibes!
